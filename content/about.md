# About

uFabric is a **non-profit organization** dedicated to the research, design, and documentation of infrastructures, practices, and tools based on artificial intelligence, with the goal of enhancing human capabilities in a responsible, verifiable, and sustainable way.

We work from an interdisciplinary perspective, prioritizing human well-being, technical transparency, and technological sovereignty.

Our work focuses on:

- generating useful and transferable knowledge
- documenting processes and technical decisions
- enabling people and organizations to build with informed judgment
- contributing to the digital public good

Areas of work:

- **applied AI research**  
Research and experimentation on how AI systems can support human cognitive processes, decision-making, learning, and collaborative work.
- **infrastructure and architecture**  
Design of technical and conceptual infrastructures that enable AI systems to be reproducible, auditable, and maintainable over time.
- **practices and frameworks**  
Development and documentation of practices that guide the responsible use of AI in real contexts, avoiding technological dependency and uncritical automation.
- **open tools**  
Creation of reusable tools and components, prioritizing openness, traceability, and local adaptability.
